Hyper Research is quality of life mod that adds a lot of useful features to the Journey mode

Features:
- Auto-researching of all items in the inventory without having to open the Research Menu every time
- Partial researching of unresearched items using the keybind
- Cleaning of all researched items in the inventory using the keybind (excluding favorites)
- Auto-researching of items that can be crafted (you need to research the necessary materials and crafting stations (or stand next to them))
- Auto-trash items that have already been researched
- Researching the contents of bags, crates and other loot items in one tap
- Researching the contents of NPC shops in one tap
- Infinite researched consumable items (divided into categories: potions, blocks, thorwing weapons, etc.)
- Messages notifying about researching new items and crafting items
- Max stack reseached items

Will be added:
- Custom unresearched items tooltip so that the tooltip will always be visible when hovering item

!The mod has not been tested in multiplayer so bugs are possible. Full-fledged multiplayer support is not planned yet!
